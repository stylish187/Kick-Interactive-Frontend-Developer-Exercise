FITTING SERVICE

To get the very best from your new kitchen, Chandlers can fit it for you too. We offer a dry fit installation service, which includes worktop fitting. By choosing us to install your kitchen we will fully project manage the installation, carried out by our experienced fitters. The work is not complete until the kitchen is signed off to your satisfaction. Our dry fit installation includes the installation of all units, trims, panels, appliances, worktops and splashbacks. You will only need to arrange plumbing, electrical connections and decoration. The fitting service is available for any number of kitchens from 1 to 100.
